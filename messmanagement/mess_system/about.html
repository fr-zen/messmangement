Our Services

Admin Login

The Admin Login facility provides authorized staff with secure access to the management dashboard. This allows administrators to efficiently oversee various aspects of the platform, such as managing user profiles, reviewing feedback, handling payment history, and controlling other critical functions. The login system is protected with robust authentication via email and password, ensuring that only authorized personnel can access sensitive data. With an intuitive interface, admins can quickly navigate through the system to make necessary updates or resolve issues promptly.

Student Login

User Registration & Login: Students and staff can log in to view or modify their meal preferences. Menu Management: Displays daily menus, allowing users to select meal options. Billing & Payments: Automates monthly mess bill calculations and payment tracking.

Transparency

Admin and student login systems create transparency by enabling real-time access to data, clear communication, and accountability. Students can track their attendance, payments, and records, while admins update and manage information. Logs and notifications ensure every action is traceable, fostering trust and minimizing disputes.
